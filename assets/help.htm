Introduction
Pocket VOA from Bin Shao brings voa text and audio to your Android-powered device. It lets you browse, stream and download.
　
Functions
Read text and play audio online, support many voa categories.
  • Standard English
    • News Program
  • Special English
    • Development Report
    • This is America
    • Agriculture Report
    • Science in the News
    • Health Report
    • Explorations
    • Education Report
    • The Making of a Nation
    • Economics Report
    • American Mosaic
    • In the News
    • American Stories
    • Words And Their Stories
    • People in America
  • English Learning
    • Popular American
　
Offline mode
Download text and audio via WIFI, and use it offline.
　
Support Chinese translation
Article with a T icon means it has Chinese translation.
　
Support Lyric
Article with a L icon means it has Lyric.
　
Screen orientation auto adaption
Auto adapt screen layout when orientation changes.
　
FAQ
Cannot get network access?
Via Menu>Network Status to check network status.
　
Settings
Max Count: max number of entries to display in remote article list.
